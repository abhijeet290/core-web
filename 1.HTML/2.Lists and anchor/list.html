How to make tea

  • Boil water in a kettle.
  • Add tea leaves or a tea bag to a cup.
  • Pour the hot water into the cup.
  • Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  • Add sugar, honey, or milk (optional).
  • Stir well and enjoy your tea!
